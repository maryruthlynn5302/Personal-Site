About Me.

I was born on May 3, 2002 in Los Angeles, California to Julie Marie Schrotenboer, now Julie Marie Eads, and Russel Lance Schrotenboer. I lived in California from birth to about 15 years old. My time spent in my home state is very important to me, even when I am visiting for vacation. I am definetly the "California Girl" type. Or I would like to hope at least that I am. I went to Woodland Hills Private School from Pre-K through Kindergarten. I attended White Oak Elementary School And Valley View Middle School, both in Simi Valley, CA before moving to Norman, Oklahoma to start my freshman year at Norman North High School. I graduated from Norman North in May of 2020, the first COVID-19 Graduaing class, and originally went to Rose State College in Midwest City, OK for Radiologic Technology.
